# Milestone v5.2: Document Revision UX

## Phase 70: History UI & Revision Tracking Fixes
**Goal:** Make the history tab closable without overlapping the navbar, and ensure document saves trigger revision history records correctly.
**Requirements:**
- REV-01: Comprehensive Revision Tracking
- REV-02: History Tab UI Fix

**Success Criteria:**
1. The history side panel has a clear 'close' or 'exit' button.
2. The history panel does not overlap or hide under the main navigation header.
3. Editing a document/blog/event and saving it successfully creates a new timestamped entry in the history panel.
